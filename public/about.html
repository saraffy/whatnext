About WhatNext

Built for people who have big goals and need a clear path forward.

What is WhatNext?

WhatNext is an intelligent goal-planning companion that transforms your ambitions into structured, actionable roadmaps. Whether you want to start a business, write a book, get fit, or change your career, WhatNext breaks the journey down into clear, manageable steps — from a single day all the way to five years. No vague advice, no generic to-do lists. Just a personalised plan built around you.

Why was it created?

Most people don't fail at their goals because they lack motivation — they fail because they don't know where to start. WhatNext was created to close the gap between ambition and action. The idea was simple: what if anyone could sit down, type out their dream, and walk away with a concrete roadmap in minutes? By combining the reasoning power of AI with an intuitive planning structure, WhatNext gives every goal the clarity it deserves.

What can it do?

WhatNext generates personalised roadmaps across ten timeframes — from 1 day to 5 years — and lets you drill into finer detail for longer plans. Each roadmap is broken into themed periods with specific, time-boxed tasks. You can track your progress as you complete each step, get AI-powered step-by-step guidance on any task, and manage multiple goals simultaneously. Every plan is built around your context — share what you know, what you've tried, and what constraints you have, and WhatNext tailors the roadmap accordingly.
